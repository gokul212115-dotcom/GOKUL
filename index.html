Who's broadcasting

I started in Android development — building IPTV and live-streaming apps with Kotlin, MVVM architecture, and ExoPlayer. Getting a streaming app right means handling buffering, network state, and user experience all at once. That combination of constraints taught me to care deeply about performance and reliability.

Over time, I expanded into full-stack web development because the same principles apply — clean architecture, fast data flow, and products that hold up under real-world conditions. Whether it's an Android TV app or a React web app, I want the foundation to be solid.
